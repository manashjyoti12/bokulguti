HOW TO PLAY

The game begins with one player picking up all of the marbles in any one of the pits on his side. Moving counter-clockwise, the player deposits one of the marbles in each pit until the marbles run out.

If the last marble, the player drops, is in his own store, then the player gets a free turn. Otherwise if the last marble ends up in his or other playerâ€™s pit,then the other player gets to play their turn.

The game ends when all five pits on one side get empty. And if the player on the other side has marbles on his pit ,then that player gets all the marbles.

In the end, the player who has most number of marbles in his or her store is the winner
