HOW TO PLAY

The game begins with one player picking up all of the marbles in any one of the holes on his side.Moving counter-clockwise, the player deposits one of the marbles in each hole until the marbles run out.

If you run into your own store, deposit one piece in it.
If the player run into the opponen's store, skip it.
If the last piece the player drops is in his own store, he get a free turn.
If the last piece the player drops is in an empty hole on his side, he capture that marble and any marblees in the hole directly opposite.
The player should always place all captured marbles in his store.

The game ends when all five spaces on one side are empty.The player who still has marbles on his side when the game ends captures all of those marbles.

Count all the marbles in each store. The winner is the player with the most marbes.
